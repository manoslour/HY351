You are considered fully vaccinated against COVID-19 if you have had:
  • 3 doses of Pfizer-BioNTech / Comirnaty, at least 21 days apart
  • 3 doses of Moderna / SpikeVax, at least 28 days apart
  • 2 doses of Johnson & Johnson's Janssen.

COVID-19 vaccine is currently not recommended for:
  • Individuals below 5 years of age for the Pfizer-BioNTech / Comirnaty vaccine
  • Individuals below 18 years of age for the Moderna / SpikeVax vaccines

Postpone your appointment if you:
  • Are unwell or had a fever in the last 24 hours
  • Are serving Stay-Home Notice (SHN) 1, Quarantine Order (QO)
  • Isolation Order (IO) 2, or received a Health Risk Warning (HRW)
